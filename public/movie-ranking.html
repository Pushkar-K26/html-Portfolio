Movies That I Recommend

Dead Silence

This is a horror movie. There are little to no jumpscares but the suspense and twist in this movie is quite a taste

Chi va Chi Sau Ka

This is a comedy Marathi movie. If you are looking for an easy laughter and have no problem with Marathi language, then give it a go

Chennai Express

Hands down the best comedy movie with a touch of romance. You can rewatch it a thousand times
